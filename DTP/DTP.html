Defend the planet

Instructions

The aliens are attacking your planet! Its your your job to stop them!


Using the gui on the right place towers on the coresponding spot.
Every tower costs metal to construct.

There are two types of towers: red and blue towers

Red tower:
  • Fire rate: 1.5
  • Range: 4
  • Damage: 3
  • Cost: 150
 Blue tower:
  • Fire rate: 3
  • Range: 5
  • Damage: 1
  • Cost: 100
There are also two types of enemies
Green enemy:
  • Health: 10
  • Speed 300
 Yelow enemy:
  • Health: 20
  • Speed 200
Once in game you have the option of changin the difficulty of the game using the drop down menu in the gui.
The higher the difficulty the higher the score gets.

The pause button pauses the game and continues the game if pressed again.

After every wave the game pauses to give you time to place towers.
Once you are ready just press the "Start wave" button for the next game.

At the bottom of the gui are music controls to pause or play the background music.

In the top right corner is the button to change your perspective of the game.
You are able to move the camera and move around using the WASD keys.

The game is won by defeating all 10 waves.

You lose if your planet health goes down to zero.
